## (Lorene’s) Vegetable Soup (8 qt pot)

**Ingredients**

* 1 lb beef
* 3 potatoes, diced
* celery
* onion
* 2 cans green beans (whole bean)
* 1 can pinto beans
* 1 lb carrots, diced
* 1 lb frozen peas
* 1 lb frozen corn
* 1 can diced tomatoes
* 8–10 beef bouillon cubes
* celery salt
* white pepper
* 2 tablespoons flour *(stir in quickly to avoid lumps)*
* water *(to desired consistency)*

**Instructions**

1. Brown the beef in a large pot.
2. Add potatoes, celery, onion, green beans, pinto beans, carrots, peas, corn, and diced tomatoes.
3. Add bouillon cubes, celery salt, and white pepper.
4. Add water to desired consistency.
5. Simmer 2+ hours.
6. Stir in flour quickly to avoid lumps.

## “Bun-steads” (Tuna Cheesies) — from Lorene Burrows

**Ingredients**

* 1/4 lb American cheese, diced *(or 1 cup American cheese, diced)*
* 3 hardcooked eggs, chopped
* 1 can tuna, drained and flaked
* 2 tablespoons green pepper, chopped
* 2 tablespoons stuffed green olives, chopped
* 2 tablespoons sweet pickles, chopped
* 1/3 cup mayonnaise
* 6 hot dog buns

**Instructions**

1. Mix the cheese, eggs, tuna, green pepper, olives, pickles, and mayonnaise lightly.
2. Fill the hot dog buns with the mixture.
3. Wrap each bun in foil.
4. Bake at 350°F for about 30 minutes, until filling is heated and cheese is melted.
5. Serve hot.

## Snickerdoodles

**Ingredients (Dough)**

* 1 cup shortening
* 1 1/2 cups sugar
* 2 eggs
* 2 3/4 cups flour
* 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
* 1 teaspoon baking soda
* 1/4 teaspoon salt

**Ingredients (Coating)**

* 2 tablespoons sugar
* 2 teaspoons cinnamon

**Instructions**

1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
2. Mix shortening and sugar.
3. Add eggs and mix.
4. Mix flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt; add to dough and mix.
5. Mix sugar and cinnamon for coating.
6. Form dough into balls and roll in coating.
7. Bake 8–10 minutes.



## Gluten-Free Peanut Butter Cookies

**Ingredients**

* 1 cup sugar
* 1 cup peanut butter
* 1 egg
* 1 tsp vanilla extract
* 1/2 cup crushed peanuts *(optional)*

**Instructions**

1. Beat sugar, peanut butter, egg, and vanilla until well blended.
2. Preheat oven to 325°F.
3. Form cookies and place on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
4. Flatten with a fork (criss-cross). Sprinkle with crushed peanuts if using.
5. Bake 10–12 minutes, or until lightly browned.
6. Cool 2 minutes on the pan.

**Makes:** about 24 cookies


## Barbecued Hamburger (Sloppy Joes) *(for 6 buns)*

**Ingredients**

* 1 lb hamburger (ground beef)
* 3/4 cup ketchup
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 large onion, cut fine *(or use minced onion, to taste)*
* 1 tablespoon vinegar
* 1 tsp sugar *(or more, to taste)*
* 1 tablespoon prepared mustard
* 6 buns

**Instructions**

1. Brown the hamburger, stirring with a fork, and partially cook. Spoon off excess fat.
2. Add ketchup, salt, onion, vinegar, sugar, and prepared mustard.
3. Simmer and/or heat about 30 minutes.

   * If using minced onion instead of fresh onion: add to taste and simmer about 10 minutes.


## Beef / Noodles

**Ingredients**

* 1 1/2 lb beef for stew
* 7 beef bouillon cubes
* water *(to cover beef)*
* salt and pepper
* onion flakes *(if desired)*
* 2 lb frozen egg noodles
* water *(to cover noodles)*

**Instructions**

1. Cook the beef until tender (about 2 hours): combine beef, bouillon cubes, and enough water to cover the beef. Season with salt and pepper; add onion flakes if desired.
2. Add frozen egg noodles and enough water to cover.
3. Cook 30–60 minutes on low, adding water as needed and stirring frequently.


## Chili Con Carne (Turkey) *(doubled)*

**Ingredients**

* 2 lb ground turkey *(ground beef is crossed out on the card)*
* 3 large onions, chopped
* 1–2 green peppers, chopped
* 1 can crushed tomatoes (1 lb 13 oz)
* 1 can tomato sauce (1 lb 13 oz)
* 1 can stewed tomatoes
* 3 lb kidney beans *(written this way on the card)*
* 3 Tbsp chili powder
* 1 1/2 Tbsp salt
* 1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
* 1/2 tsp paprika
* 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
* 3 cloves garlic, crushed
* 3 bay leaves
* 1/8 tsp “G. parsley leaves” *(as written)*

**Instructions**

1. Brown the meat.
2. Add onions and green peppers; cook 2–3 minutes.
3. Pour off excess fat.
4. Add the rest of the ingredients.
5. Simmer 1 hour.
6. Remove bay leaves.

## Chicken Roll-ups *(from Amy Harris)*

**Ingredients**

* About 1/2 lb cooked chicken, cut into small pieces *(or 1 can chicken, drained — noted as “13 oz can (drained)”)*
* 1 can cream of chicken soup
* 1/2 can milk *(use the soup can to measure)*
* 8 oz Velveeta *(or 1 cup shredded cheddar)*
* Crescent rolls

**Instructions**

1. Cut chicken into small pieces.
2. Mix soup, milk, and cheese; cook until cheese is melted.
3. Pour 1/2 of the sauce into the bottom of a baking dish.
4. Put some chicken on the wide end of a crescent roll, roll up, and place on the sauce.
5. Repeat for all rolls. Cover with the rest of the sauce.
6. Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes.



## Chicken-Rice Casserole *(from Betty Crocker cookbook — modified)*

*(see book for microwave option)*
*(use 3 qt pans)*
**Serves:** 6

**Ingredients**

* 1/4 cup margarine (4 Tbsp) **or** butter
* 1/3 cup all-purpose flour *(or self-rising)*
* 1/8 tsp pepper
* optional: 1 1/2 tsp salt *(might be too salty — add to taste)*
* 1 cup chicken broth
* 1 1/2 cups milk
* 1 1/2 cups cooked white **or** wild rice
* 2 cups cut-up cooked chicken *(or turkey)*
* optional: mushrooms, green pepper, pimiento, almonds *(see cookbook)*
* optional: parsley *(for garnish)*

**Instructions**

1. Heat margarine (or butter) in a 2-qt saucepan until melted.
2. Blend in flour and pepper (and salt if desired).
3. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly until bubbly. Remove from heat.
4. Stir in chicken broth and milk.
5. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly. Boil and stir 1 minute.
6. Stir in cooked rice and cut-up cooked chicken (or turkey). Add optional mushrooms/green pepper/pimiento/almonds if using.
7. Pour into an ungreased 2-qt casserole or oblong baking dish (about 10 x 6 x 1 1/2 inches).
8. Bake uncovered in a 350°F oven until bubbly, 40–45 minutes.
9. Garnish with parsley if desired.

## Holiday Potatoes

**Ingredients**

* 3 lb potatoes, peeled and quartered, cooked
* 1/2 cup butter
* 6 oz cream cheese
* 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese, divided
* 2 oz diced pimento, drained
* 1 small green pepper, chopped
* 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
* 1/4 cup milk
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 bunch green onions, chopped

**Instructions**

1. Mash the cooked potatoes.
2. Add butter and cream cheese; beat until smooth.
3. Add 1/2 cup cheddar and the remaining ingredients; mix.
4. Spoon into a lightly greased 9x13 baking dish.
5. If refrigerated, remove from fridge for 30 minutes before baking.
6. Bake at 350°F for 35 minutes.
7. Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup cheddar.
8. Bake until cheese melts.

## Mac + Cheese

**Ingredients**

* 4 cups macaroni, cooked and drained
* 3/4 lb Velveeta
* some milk
* salt and pepper

**Instructions**

1. Cook 4 cups macaroni. Drain.
2. Add 3/4 lb Velveeta, some milk, and salt and pepper.
3. Heat on low until smooth.
4. Let stand for 1 hour **or** bake at 350°F for about 30 minutes.

**Note:** *(as written on card)* “10 cups mac = 1 1/2 lb Velveeta”
