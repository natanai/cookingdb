1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (177°C). Peel and cut {{sweet_potatoes}}.
2. Boil {{sweet_potatoes}} in salted water until almost tender, then drain well.
3. Spread {{sweet_potatoes}} in a greased casserole dish. Sprinkle with {{salt}}, {{brown_sugar}}, and {{flour}}, then pour {{cream}} over the top.
4. Bake for 20–30 minutes, or until the sauce is sticky and bubbling around the edges.
